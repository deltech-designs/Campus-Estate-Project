Everything You Need

A complete digital solution for hostel life — from application to maintenance.

🏠

Smart Room Allocation

State-based preference matching ensures you share a room with compatible flatmates from the same state or a mix of states.

📋

Online Room Requests

Apply for a hostel room anytime from any device. Track your request status in real-time — Pending, Approved, or Rejected.

🔧

Maintenance Complaints

Report broken doors, electrical faults, and plumbing issues directly online. Track each complaint through to resolution.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Administrators can approve requests, allocate rooms, and manage the entire hostel portfolio from a single control panel.

🔒

Secure & Private

All passwords are securely hashed. Sessions are protected. Your personal data stays safe at all times.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design — works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops without any app download.

How It Works

Four simple steps to get your hostel room.

01

Register

Create your student account with your matric number and personal details.

02

Set Preference

Choose Same State, Mixed, or No Preference for your room allocation.

03

Request Room

Submit your room request and wait for admin approval notification.

04

Move In

Once approved, view your assigned room details and move in!
